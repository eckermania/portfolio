About Me

Erin has worked for one of the world's largest companies and smallest non-profits. After building up a skillset of program evaluation and program management skills for over a decade, she decided it was time to learn how to build. This was her prompt for attending the coding bootcamp at Code Fellows in Seattle and developing websites and applications - providing user-friendly retail sites, access to information about social services, and games that delight.
